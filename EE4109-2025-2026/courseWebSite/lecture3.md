## Overview
This lecture covers MOS transistor modeling using the EKV model for the EE4109 course at TU Delft.

## Key Topics

**MOS EKV Model:**
The EKV model offers an "all-regions nonlinear dynamic model" with "active forward region small-signal and stationary noise model" capabilities. Unlike BSIM models optimized for simulation speed, the EKV approach "shows a strong relation with the physical operation of the device," enabling designers to connect small-signal parameters to device geometry and technology specifications.

The inversion coefficient serves as a critical design parameter correlating device operating conditions to performance metrics.
