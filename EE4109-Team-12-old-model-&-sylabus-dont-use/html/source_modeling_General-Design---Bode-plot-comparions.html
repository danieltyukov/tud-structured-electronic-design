General Design - Bode plot comparions

When a MOS enters triode region, it's C_iss increases due to a 'larger channel'. In Saturation the C_iss is approximately 2/3 of C_ox

When a Mos enters triode region, it's gm decreases. In saturation the gm saturates, so dont put to much current because you dont gain gm

The Ft follows gm, because in saturation the C_iss is constant

\begin{equation} f_{T}=\frac{0.5 g_{m}}{\pi C_{iss}} \end{equation}
